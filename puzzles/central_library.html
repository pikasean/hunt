Central Library
Stumped after wasting yet another hour trying to crack your old account's password, you decided to take a break, and headed to the Central Library. While checking the ISBN of your required textbook, a sheet of paper fell in front of you. Puzzled, you decided to give it a garner, and noticed a short list of numbers. Suddenly, words of your Divination lecturer ran through your head, reminding you that to read through the future, one must use the context and actions of the past to aid you. Maybe this sheet of numbers can solve your password dilemma?
