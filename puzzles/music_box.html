Music Box
While walking around the music conservatory, you noticed an interactive art piece, consisting of several music boxes placed next to each other. In front of it, a sign reads, "Precious as it may seem, yet fleeting as it may be." Fiddling with the music boxes, you suddenly get a sense of nostalgia. What's more, you found out that each of these music boxes contain a different set of scenic photographs that awfully fit each music boxes' lullaby. Could this sense of familiarity help you solve the riddle?
