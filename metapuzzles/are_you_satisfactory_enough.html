Are You Satisfactory Enough?
You think you’ve managed to go back to reality… but you’re transported into an alternate dimension yet again that transcends space and time. Founding headmaster, Al-NUS Dumbledore, appears and presents to you a list of modules that you should have taken during your time in Alt-NUS, and says that without these modules, you stand no chance to unlock the portal back to your reality. “As you can see, you are unsatisfactory, but my ass is!” What incantation do you say to Alnus that proves that you have mastered what it takes?
Note: To solve this puzzle, you will need answers from all 7 metapuzzles and all 3 event puzzles (under USC). In the real camp, this puzzle will be hidden and only released on the last day for the freshies to solve in real time. For the dry run, we are keeping it open.
