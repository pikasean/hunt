There are three base-ball playing witches in Alt-NUS: Blossom, Bubbles and Buttercup. Contrary to popular belief that base-ball is a friendly, safe and boring sport, these three witches have a habit of making the affair bloody. Because of this, Blossom turned colour blind after an accident. What is now her favourite colour?
(The flavour text is all you need to solve this.)
To solve this metapuzzle, you will need 3 answers from the Faculty of Science, and 3 answers from the Music Conservatory. No answer is used in more than one metapuzzle.
