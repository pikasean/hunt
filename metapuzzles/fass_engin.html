Remus the wizard wants to brew a potion, but he’s having trouble getting that one last ingredient. Since he is part-werewolf, whenever he goes on a search, he must turn away when a full moon appears. When he does, he loses sight of the ingredient after a few steps, so he turns and continues forward on his journey, but another full moon always shows up without fail (This reality has absurdly many moons). This process repeats, and perplexingly enough, Remus ends up where he started after a full search. Alas, full moons are indeed not Remus’ best friend, crescent moons are. You intend to help him with his search. What is the last ingredient?
To solve this metapuzzle, you will need 3 answers from the Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences, and 3 answers from the Faculty of Engineering. No answer is used in more than one metapuzzle.
