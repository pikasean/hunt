Guide to the Hunt
Oh no...

You've been transported to an alternate reality where magic exists. The university seems the same, except that different subjects are taught in the faculties now. Visit each faculty to solve puzzles and learn new spells.
Prove your worth to the wizards and find your way back to reality! Head over to the map to start your journey!

Important
  • Some puzzle clues will be given out during our online information sessions, so try to attend them if possible!
  • We scattered a few clues to the puzzles around this site.
  • The final puzzle will be conducted live and the link will be given later on.
  • The features of this site have primarily been tested with Chrome. It is best viewed on a laptop/desktop PC and not a phone.
  • If you've found an error in a puzzle or a link is broken, contact us at this email. You can also email us for clarifications. If there are any changes to the puzzles, it will be updated at the errata page.
