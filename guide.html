Guide to the Hunt
Oh no...

You've been transported to an alternate reality where magic exists. The university seems the same, except that different subjects are taught in the faculties now. Visit each faculty to solve puzzles and learn new spells.
Prove your worth to the wizards and find your way back to reality! Head over to the map to start your journey!

  • QET Briefing
  • CCA Sharing
Submissions
After logging in, you will able to submit the answer for each puzzle on the respective puzzle pages. Please contact your OGL for the Username and Password. Some important points to note:
  • Only one person from each team needs to submit the answer for each puzzle.
  • Submit your answer using only the characters a-zA-Z.
  • After each submission, you should receive one of 3 responses. ("Correct", "Incorrect", or "Your team has already solved this")
Hints
For the dry run, each team will be given 5 free hints. Each additional hint on top of that will cost 0.50 SGD. (We are collecting funds for prizes for the actual camp. The hint system in the actual camp will be different.) Details about requesting hints are as follows:
  • Send an email here titled "(Team Name) - Hint Request for (Puzzle Name).
  • Please do not request for hints using a team name that is not yours.
  • Please inform your teammates that you are requesting for a hint.
  • You can be assured that if more than 1 team requests a hint for the same puzzle, they will receive the same hint.
Scoring
We use a simple scoring system for this puzzle hunt. The leaderboard can be found here . Details are as follows:
  • Each puzzle is worth 1 point.
  • Each metapuzzle is worth 6 points.
  • The final puzzle (metametapuzzle) is worth 10 points.
Technical Issues
The features of this site have primarily been tested with Chrome. It is best viewed on a laptop/desktop PC and not a phone. If you've found an error in a puzzle or a link is broken, contact us at this email. You can also email us for clarifications.
